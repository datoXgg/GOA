About ASUS

ASUS is a leading global technology company known for high-quality products and innovations. With a focus on design and performance, ASUS has been a pioneer in the tech industry for decades.

Founded in 1989, ASUS started as a motherboard manufacturer and has since expanded its product range to include laptops, desktops, graphics cards, monitors, and more.

Products

Laptops

ASUS laptops are synonymous with innovation, combining cutting-edge technology with elegant design. The ZenBook series, designed for professionals, not only boasts NanoEdge displays but also incorporates innovative features like ScreenPad technology, transforming the touchpad into an interactive secondary display. On the gaming front, the ROG (Republic of Gamers) series introduces features like high-refresh-rate displays, advanced cooling systems, and customizable RGB lighting, providing an immersive gaming experience. ASUS TUF series offers a balance between performance and durability, making it an ideal choice for gamers seeking reliability.

Motherboards

ASUS motherboards are the backbone of customized PC builds. The Prime series, known for reliability, features robust power delivery and extensive connectivity options. The ROG Strix series, designed for gamers and enthusiasts, integrates advanced cooling solutions, PCIe 4.0 support, and AI-powered overclocking. The ProArt series caters to content creators, offering exceptional color accuracy and connectivity for creative work. ASUS workstation motherboards, under the WS series, prioritize stability and expandability, making them suitable for professional use.

Graphics Cards

The ASUS graphics card lineup spans a spectrum of user needs. The ROG Strix graphics cards, equipped with Axial-tech fans and MaxContact technology, deliver top-tier cooling performance for gaming enthusiasts. The TUF Gaming series emphasizes durability with features like Auto-Extreme Technology for precise manufacturing. The Dual series provides a balance between performance and affordability, making it accessible for a broader audience. ASUS also ventures into specialized graphics cards, such as the ROG Matrix series, which focuses on extreme overclocking potential for enthusiasts pushing the limits of performance.
